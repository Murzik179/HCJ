align

The align attribute - standard attribute defining text alignment in the document.

Values:

  • Left - the text is aligned to the left edge
  • Center - text is centered
  • Right - the text is aligned to the right edge
  • Justify - width alignment

Tag Usage:

  • <h1>
  • <h2>
  • <h3>
  • <h4>
  • <h5>
  • <h6>

Example:

<p align="center">Hello, World!</p>
